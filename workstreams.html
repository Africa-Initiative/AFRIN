Our Workstreams

Driving Africa’s Development Through Strategic Focus Areas

Our Strategic Workstreams

Workstream 1: Aligning Research Agendas to Socio-Economic Priorities

We enhance UM6P’s research for development (R4D) impact by connecting existing research efforts to Africa’s socio-economic needs and the SDGs. Our role is to facilitate collaboration between UM6P departments and external stakeholders to ensure research addresses critical challenges like food security and sustainable energy. By curating opportunities for alignment, we strengthen UM6P’s responsiveness to continental demands.

Workstream 2: Envisioning African Futures in Education and Innovation

We aspire to become a think tank that positions UM6P as a leader in shaping the future of African higher education, science, and entrepreneurship. We coordinate visionary discussions among UM6P faculty, departments, and external partners to explore emerging trends and solutions for socio-economic development. Our efforts focus on amplifying UM6P’s intellectual influence by fostering interdepartmental synergy and laying the groundwork for strategic insights that inspire action across the continent.

Workstream 3: Fostering Academic Excellence Through Partnerships and Networks

We strengthen UM6P’s academic leadership by facilitating strategic partnerships and networks across Africa and beyond. By coordinating interdepartmental efforts within UM6P, we connect the academic community with opportunities to address continental challenges. Our work builds a collaborative ecosystem that enhances excellence, linking UM6P with institutions and stakeholders to share knowledge and drive impact.

Workstream 4: Connecting Innovation to Entrepreneurship

In collaboration with UM6P’s entrepreneurial ecosystem (e.g., Stargate incubator), we strengthen UM6P’s role in driving innovation and entrepreneurship. We facilitate connections between innovators, researchers, and external partners to address Africa’s socio-economic challenges. By coordinating access to resources and fostering synergy across departments, we enable impactful solutions to emerge and scale, reinforcing UM6P’s leadership in actionable change.

Workstream 5: Communication and Outreach

We amplify the impact of our initiatives by fostering communication and outreach across Africa, engaging stakeholders through events, publications, and digital platforms to ensure our work reaches and benefits communities continent-wide.
