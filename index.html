Mission & Workstreams

The Africa Initiative is dedicated to contributing to Africa’s socio-economic development. We support collaboration, foster dialogue around innovative futures, and help strengthen UM6P’s role in higher education, research for development (R4D), innovation, and entrepreneurship across the continent.

Strategic Partnerships

Facilitating networks with African and global institutions to support UM6P’s contributions and extend its reach.

Innovation Hub

Encouraging visionary ideas and dialogue to help shape Africa’s future through UM6P’s ecosystem.

Continental Focus

Engaging with Africa’s challenges through collaborative, localized solutions.
