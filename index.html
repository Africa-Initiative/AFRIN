Welcome to the UM6P - Africa Initiative

Based at UM6P in Benguerir, Morocco, the Africa Initiative Department is dedicated to advancing Africa’s socio-economic development. We facilitate collaboration, envision innovative futures, and strengthen UM6P’s leadership in higher education, research for development (R4D), innovation, and entrepreneurship across the continent.

Our Mission

Strengthen UM6P’s leading position in higher education, R4D, innovation, and entrepreneurship through strategic partnerships and networks, OCP action on the ground, and interdepartmental coordination for responsiveness to the socio-economic development needs of Africa.

Our Achievements

Collaborations Across 20+ African Countries

We’ve established research partnerships with institutions in over 20 African countries, fostering a continent-wide network to address socio-economic challenges.

10+ Agricultural Innovations Supported

In collaboration with OCP Group, we’ve supported 10+ innovations in sustainable agriculture, impacting rural communities across Africa through improved farming practices.

50+ Research Institutions Engaged

Our initiatives have engaged 50+ research institutions across Africa and globally, driving academic excellence and knowledge exchange through programs like “100 PhDs for Africa.”
